Guardian FSD Booster

The Guardian FSD Booster is a valuable module for any explorer.

Installing the Guardian FSD Booster on a ship can provide up to a 10ly boost in jump range and most CMDR's obtain the necessary materials to unlock the Guardian FSD Booster in a few hours.

To start with you'll want to equip 1-2 point defense modules on the TOP of your ship, and install an SRV bay with two SRV's. Also, make sure you have plenty of materials for SRV ammunnition, refeuling, and repair.

A complete list of materials needed is avalable from Inara

The guide

The map below is for the "Guardian Structure" located on Synuefe NL-N C23-4, Planet B3

Guardian Module Blueprint Map - Synuefe NL-N C23-4 B3

This guide is "Work In Progress", and will be exanded upon in the near future. The basics are as follows:

  • Obtain an Ancient Relic from a tower
  • Activate (but do not power up) all six pylons
  • Shoot down (and collect wreckage from) any guardian sentinels that spawn
  • Shoot (and collect materials from) any "destructible panels" located around the site
  • Scan obelisks with SRV data scanner (and collect data materials)
  • Power up all six pylons by shooting the blue orb in the center with SRV turret
  • (Quickly) defeat any remaining guardian sentinels
  • Eject the Ancient Relic from the SRV onto the "socket" near the Guardian Data Terminal
  • Scan (with SRV data scanner) the Guardian Data Terminal
  • Farm obelisks for data materials, and shoot destrutible panels as needed for Guardian Technology Components
