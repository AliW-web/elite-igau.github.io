Guardian FSD Booster

The Guardian FSD Booster is a valuable module for any explorer. Installing the Guardian FSD Booster on a ship can provide up to a 10ly boost in jump range. Most CMDR's obtain the necessary materials to unlock the Guardian FSD Booster in a few hours. You'll want to equip 1-2 point defense modules on the TOP of your ship, and install an SRV bay with TWO SRV's. Additionally, make sure you have plenty of materials for SRV ammunnition, refuling, and repair.

The complete list of materials needed is avalable from Inara

There are numerous Guardian locations where the "Module Blueprint" can be obtained, however this guide, and the accompanying map are for the "Guardian Structure" located on Synuefe NL-N C23-4, Planet B3
